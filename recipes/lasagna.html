Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a type of pasta that is wide and flat, and possibly one of the oldest types of pasta. It is made with layers of pasta, cheese, meat, and sauce.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Ground beef
  • Marinara sauce
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Eggs
  • Italian seasoning

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, eggs, and Italian seasoning.
  4. In a baking dish, layer noodles, ricotta mixture, ground beef, marinara sauce, and mozzarella cheese.
  5. Repeat layers until all ingredients are used.
  6. Top with Parmesan cheese.
  7. Bake for 45 minutes or until bubbly and golden brown.
